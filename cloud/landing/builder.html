    WHAT IS A PASSPORT?
    QU'EST-CE QU'UN PASSEPORT?
    
The identity layer your agents are missing

    
La couche d'identite qui manque a vos agents

    
Without a passport, your agent is anonymous. Nobody knows what it can do, what it's allowed to do, or what it actually did. AIB fixes that.

    
Sans passeport, votre agent est anonyme. Personne ne sait ce qu'il peut faire, ce qu'il est autorise a faire, ni ce qu'il a fait. AIB resout ca.

  


  

    

      
🎟

      
Cryptographic Identity

      
Identite cryptographique

      
Every agent gets a unique URN and an Ed25519 key pair. Its identity is verifiable by any third party without calling your API — offline, forever.

      
Chaque agent obtient un URN unique et une paire de cles Ed25519. Son identite est verifiable par tout tiers sans appeler votre API — hors ligne, pour toujours.

      
urn:aib:agent:acme:invoice-bot
signed_by: 314eff...aa9a
tier: permanent

    

    

      
⚖

      
Embedded Policies

      
Politiques embarquees

      
Policies travel with the passport — signed and tamper-proof. Rate limits, capability restrictions, approval gates: enforced before every action, not as an afterthought.

      
Les politiques voyagent avec le passeport — signees et inviolables. Limites de debit, restrictions de capacites, validations: appliquees avant chaque action.

      
rate_limit: 100/hour ✓
deliverable_gate: >500EUR ✓
separation_of_duties ✓

    

    

      
✅

      
Portable & Auditable

      
Portable et auditable

      
Every action generates a signed receipt: who acted, what was done, when, on which data, with what authorization. Article 12 EU AI Act compliant out of the box.

      
Chaque action genere un recu signe: qui a agi, quoi, quand, sur quelles donnees, avec quelle autorisation. Conforme Article 12 EU AI Act sans configuration.

      
receipt: ed25519:7f3c9a...
intent: invoice_validation
risk_level: medium

    

  


  
  

    USE CASES
    CAS D'USAGE
    
Any agent that acts autonomously needs a passport

    
Tout agent qui agit de facon autonome a besoin d'un passeport

    
AI agents, RPA robots, CI/CD pipelines, chatbots — if it makes decisions, it needs an identity.

    
Agents IA, robots RPA, pipelines CI/CD, chatbots — s'il prend des decisions, il a besoin d'une identite.

  


  

    

      
🧠

      
AI Agents

      
Agents IA

      
LangChain, CrewAI, OpenAI Agents SDK, Google ADK. Multi-agent pipelines where agents delegate to each other — every hop is traced.

      
LangChain, CrewAI, OpenAI Agents SDK, Google ADK. Pipelines multi-agents ou les agents se deleguent les taches — chaque etape est tracee.

      MCP + A2A
    

    

      
🤖

      
RPA Robots

      
Robots RPA

      
UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Blue Prism, Power Automate. Your bots validate invoices, process HR requests, approve payments — without a verifiable identity, you can't prove what they did.

      
UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Blue Prism, Power Automate. Vos bots valident des factures, traitent des dossiers RH — sans identite verifiable, impossible de prouver ce qu'ils ont fait.

      Article 12 required
    

    

      
🔧

      
CI/CD Agents

      
Agents CI/CD

      
GitHub Actions, Jenkins, ArgoCD. Agents that deploy to production, run tests, modify infrastructure. Policy gates ensure no agent deploys without approval.

      
GitHub Actions, Jenkins, ArgoCD. Agents qui deploient en production, executent des tests, modifient l'infrastructure. Les gates de politique garantissent qu'aucun agent ne deploie sans approbation.

      separation_of_duties
    

    

      
💬

      
Chatbots & Copilots

      
Chatbots & Copilots

      
Customer-facing agents, internal copilots, support bots. If they access personal data or take actions on behalf of users, EU AI Act transparency obligations apply.

      
Agents client-facing, copilotes internes, bots de support. S'ils accdent a des donnees personnelles ou agissent pour des utilisateurs, les obligations EU AI Act s'appliquent.

      Limited risk

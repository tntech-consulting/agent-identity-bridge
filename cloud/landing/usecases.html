Berkmont Capital Group

Investment Bank · €240B AUM · Paris / London

Berkmont operates 23 RPA agents across its securities settlement and compliance functions, processing €4.2B in daily transaction volume. With MiFID II, EMIR and MAR obligations, every automated action must be traceable, timestamped, and signed — with zero tolerance for conflicts of interest between detection and reporting functions.

⏱
T2S Market Hours — Settlement instructions blocked outside 07:00–18:00 UTC. Post-market automation freezes to prevent cascading settlement failures.
⚖
MAR Separation of Duties — No single agent can both screen for suspicious patterns AND generate regulatory reports. Article 16 MiFID II compliance.
📊
Basel III Volume Control — Max 3 automated settlement initiations per 10 minutes per agent. Excess requires human sign-off.
🔒
ESMA Permanent Tier Only — Regulatory submissions (EMIR, SFTR) restricted to permanent-tier agents with verified Ed25519 identities.

Rydell Health Systems

Hospital Group · 47 Clinics · Germany / Netherlands

Rydell deploys AI agents for clinical documentation, patient triage assistance and pharmaceutical ordering. Operating under GDPR, HDS (Hébergeur de Données de Santé), and MDR (Medical Device Regulation), their agents must never access patient data without explicit consent tracing, and diagnostic assistance agents are strictly prohibited from autonomous prescription decisions.

👥
Patient Data Isolation — Agents accessing PHI (Protected Health Information) are scoped to a single patient session. Cross-patient data access is hard-blocked at the passport level.
💊
No Autonomous Prescriptions — Any agent with diagnostic_assist capability is blocked from prescription_create. MDR Annex I safety requirement.
🕐
Night-mode Restrictions — Non-emergency automated patient contact (reminders, surveys) is blocked between 22:00–07:00 local. GDPR recital 47.
⏳
Consent Verification Gate — Every PHI access requires a signed consent attestation in the receipt. Missing consent = action denied with Article 9 GDPR flag.

Nexara Networks

Telco Operator · 18M Subscribers · Europe

Nexara runs AI agents for network anomaly detection, customer churn prediction, and automated billing dispute resolution. Their AI Act classification places churn prediction in “limited risk” (transparency obligations) and network security agents in “high risk” (Article 12 logging). Automated customer contact agents must respect GDPR opt-out lists and DSP2 authentication rules.

📰
Churn Contact Throttle — Customer outreach agents max 50 automated contacts/hour. Above this threshold, a human queue manager must review. GDPR Article 21 (right to object).
🔓
Network Agent Isolation — Security scanning agents cannot access billing or CRM data. Capability chain validation prevents lateral access escalation.
📝
Dispute Resolution Limit — Automated billing refunds capped at €500 per session per agent. Above this, mandatory human escalation via deliverable_gate.
⌛
EU AI Act Transparency — Churn prediction outputs must include a signed explanation receipt identifying which data points triggered the prediction. Article 13 obligation.

Orbital Aerostructures

Aerospace MRO · Defence Certified · Toulouse

Orbital uses AI agents across MRO (Maintenance, Repair & Overhaul) documentation, supply chain ordering, and technical compliance checking for Airworthiness Directives. Operating under EASA Part-M, DO-326A cyber, and NATO STANAG 4671, their agents must never sign airworthiness documents autonomously — human co-signature is mandatory. Export-controlled data (ITAR/EAR) requires agent identity verification before any technical document access.

🛠
No Autonomous Airworthiness Sign-off — Agents with mro_document_generate are permanently blocked from airworthiness_sign. EASA Part-M requires licensed engineer co-signature.
🏴
ITAR Export Control Gate — Technical documentation agents must present a valid export license attestation in their passport before accessing ITAR-classified drawings. Session agents blocked entirely.
🆕
Change-freeze Windows — During scheduled maintenance windows (Sat 02:00–06:00 UTC), all supply chain ordering agents are frozen. DO-326A change management.
📋
Traceability Chain — Every action on a work order must include the full invocation chain (human > orchestrator > agent). Broken chains are denied with STANAG 4671 flag.

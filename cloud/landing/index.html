AIB (Agent Identity Bridge) est un protocole ouvert d'identite cryptographique pour les agents IA. Il fournit a chaque agent un passeport unique signe Ed25519, permettant l'authentification, la verification et la tracabilite sur toutes les plateformes. C'est un standard ouvert, pas un produit IAM proprietaire.AIB (Agent Identity Bridge) is an open cryptographic identity protocol for AI agents. It gives each agent a unique Ed25519-signed passport, enabling authentication, verification and traceability across all platforms. It's an open standard, not a proprietary IAM product.
Sans identite verifiable, impossible de savoir quel agent a fait quoi, quand, et avec quelles permissions. AIB attribue a chaque agent un passeport cryptographique unique qui permet de l'authentifier, de tracer ses actions et de prouver sa conformite — essentiel pour la gouvernance, l'audit et la confiance entre systemes.Without a verifiable identity, there's no way to know which agent did what, when, and with which permissions. AIB gives each agent a unique cryptographic passport to authenticate it, trace its actions and prove compliance — essential for governance, audit and trust between systems.
Oui. AIB integre la conformite EU AI Act par design, en couvrant les articles 9 (gestion des risques), 12 (logging), 13 (transparence), 14 (controle humain) et 17 (systeme qualite). AIB integrates EU AI Act compliance by design, covering articles 9 (risk management), 12 (logging), 13 (transparency), 14 (human oversight) and 17 (quality system). An automated compliance agent generates bilingual FR/EN reports.
Chaque passeport agent est signe avec Ed25519. Chaque operation (creation, verification, traduction) genere un receipt cryptographique immutable qui constitue une preuve verifiable. Every operation (create, verify, translate) generates an immutable cryptographic receipt as verifiable proof. Keys are managed server-side with automatic rotation.

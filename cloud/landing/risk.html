4 risk levels, real consequences

4 niveaux de risque, des consequences reelles

🚫
Unacceptable Risk
Risque inacceptable
Banned outright. Social scoring, real-time biometric surveillance in public spaces, cognitive manipulation. Fine: up to 7% of global turnover.
Interdit. Notation sociale, surveillance biometrique en temps reel dans les espaces publics, manipulation cognitive. Amende: jusqu'a 7% du CA mondial.
⚠
High Risk
Risque eleve
Strict obligations. Article 12 logs, Article 14 human oversight, conformity assessments. Fine: up to 3% of global turnover.
Obligations strictes. Journaux Article 12, supervision humaine Article 14, evaluations de conformite. Amende: jusqu'a 3% du CA mondial.
💡
Limited Risk
Risque limite
Transparency obligations. Users must be informed they're interacting with an AI. Deepfakes and generated content must be labeled. Fine: up to 1.5%.
Obligations de transparence. Les utilisateurs doivent etre informes. Les deepfakes doivent etre labellises. Amende: jusqu'a 1,5%.
✅
Minimal Risk
Risque minimal
No mandatory requirements. Most spam filters, recommendation systems, AI in video games. Voluntary codes of conduct encouraged.
Pas d'exigences obligatoires. La plupart des filtres anti-spam, systemes de recommandation. Codes de conduite volontaires encourages.

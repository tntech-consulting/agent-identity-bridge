How to read this page

Uptime is the percentage of successful health checks over the last 24 hours. We ping each API endpoint every hour — if it responds with a valid status code, it counts as healthy. 99%+ is excellent.

Avg Latency is the average response time across all endpoints. Under 500ms is excellent, 500–2000ms is normal for Edge Functions with cold starts, over 2000ms may indicate load.

Alerts are triggered when an endpoint goes down or takes more than 5 seconds to respond. The ops team is notified instantly by email at noreply@aib-tech.fr.
